How it works

LeadLoop for Businesses is designed to make chatbot creation simple, especially for teams that want a polished result without starting from scratch.

1. Start from your website or business details

  • Paste in your website URL
  • Or use the guided builder
  • Add service details, FAQs, contact info, and goals

2. Generate your chatbot

  • Draft a business assistant from your content
  • Set tone, messaging, and next steps
  • Shape the assistant around your business goals

3. Preview the experience

  • Test real customer-style questions
  • Refine the responses and flow
  • Adjust branding and launcher text

4. Publish on your website

  • Generate a widget snippet
  • Embed the chatbot on your site
  • Start supporting visitors right away
