How LeadLoop works

No matter which path you choose, LeadLoop is designed to make the build process simple, visual, and easy to test before launch.

1. Add your content

Paste a website URL, upload a PDF, or use the guided builder to define your chatbot.

2. Generate the chatbot

LeadLoop turns your content into a chatbot draft with messaging, FAQs, and a usable assistant flow.

3. Preview and publish

Test the chatbot experience, make refinements, and generate an install-ready widget.
