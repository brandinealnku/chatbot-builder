How it works

LeadLoop for Courses is designed to make chatbot setup simple for instructors and teams.

1. Upload your materials

  • Upload a syllabus PDF
  • Add assignment instructions or policy docs
  • Include office hours, FAQs, or course notes

2. Generate your course assistant

  • LeadLoop extracts content from your documents
  • Drafts chatbot messaging and FAQs
  • Creates a previewable support chatbot

3. Preview and refine

  • Test student-style questions
  • Adjust tone and key guidance
  • Review how the assistant responds

4. Publish and share

  • Embed on a course page or support site
  • Share as a course assistant resource
  • Give students a clearer support experience
